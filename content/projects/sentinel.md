**Sentinel** is a job orchestration system in C# inspired by Autosys and it's designed to be a modern, flexible, and efficient solution for managing and scheduling jobs in various environments. Sentinel provides a robust framework for defining, scheduling, and monitoring jobs, making it easier for developers and system administrators to automate tasks and workflows.
