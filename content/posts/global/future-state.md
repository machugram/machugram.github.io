## My Manifesto for a Less Annoying World 🌍

*Or: How I'd Fix Everything if I Were Benevolent Dictator for a Day*

### The Rent-Before-Tax Revolution

Here's a wild idea: What if we treated housing like the basic human need it is? Picture this - you pay your rent FIRST, then the government gets to play with what's left over. 

Currently, Uncle Patrick is like that friend who orders the most expensive thing on the menu when you're splitting the bill. He takes his cut before you even see your paycheck, leaving you to figure out how to afford a roof over your head with whatever crumbs remain.

**My proposed law:** Rent payments are deducted before any tax calculations. Live in a £2000/month apartment? Congrats, that comes off the top before anyone starts calculating what you "owe" society.

*Side effect: Politicians might actually care about housing costs when their tax revenue depends on people having money left after paying rent.*

### The UBI Tax Shield

And while we're at it, let's make Universal Basic Income truly universal by making it untouchable. You know how billionaires have offshore accounts? Well, regular people should have an "onshore account" - that basic income needed for survival should be completely tax-free.

**The rule:** Whatever amount your country deems necessary for basic survival gets the same tax treatment as a trust fund baby's inheritance - completely protected.

### Why This Would Actually Work

1. **Rent-first policy** = Landlords can't just jack up prices knowing renters will "find a way"
2. **UBI protection** = People can actually plan their lives without doing tax calculus
3. **Everybody wins** = Even the government, because stressed-out broke people don't exactly boost the economy

### Countries I'm Looking At You 👀


**Ireland:** Your rent is so high, even leprechauns are considering moving to cheaper pastures. Time to fix that.

**United States:** You've got tech bros living in $5000/month closets. Fix this.

**United Kingdom:** Your housing situation makes Monopoly look affordable.

**Canada:** Even your igloos are overpriced now.

**Australia:** When spiders are the *least* scary thing about your cost of living, we have a problem.

The best part? These aren't radical communist ideas - they're just common sense with better accounting. But hey, I guess that's what makes them revolutionary. 🤷‍♂️
